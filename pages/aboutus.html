TvVilla-Your Complete guide to TV Shows

The Television-Villa(abbreviated TvVilla) is an online database of information related to television programs including cast, production crew, fictional characters, biographies, plot summaries, trivia and reviews.

TvVilla is the world's most popular and authoritative source for TV and celebrity content. The TvVilla consumer site (www.tvvilla.com) is the #1 tv website in the world with a combined web and mobile audience of more than 200 million unique monthly visitors.

TvVilla offers a searchable database of more than 180 million data items with TV and entertainment programs and more than 6 million cast and crew members.

Consumers rely on the information TvVilla provides -- including local movie showtimes, ticketing, trailers, critic and user reviews, personalized recommendations, photo galleries, entertainment news, quotes, trivia, box-office data, editorial feature sections and a universal Watchlist - when deciding what to watch and where to watch it.

The database had been expanded to include additional categories of tvmakers and other demographic material, as well as trivia, biographies, and plot summaries. The movie ratings had been properly integrated with the list data and a centralized email interface for querying the database had been created by Shubham Kumar.
